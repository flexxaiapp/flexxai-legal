Quick Summary: Flexx AI is a fitness and nutrition app. We collect only the data needed to provide personalized fitness and nutrition guidance. 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Flexx AI ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use the Flexx AI mobile application ("App") and related services ("Services").

Flexx AI is developed and operated by an independent developer. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for authentication and account communications
  • Name — used to personalize your experience
  • Password — securely hashed; we never store or have access to your plaintext password
  • Authentication provider data — if you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email from the provider (we never receive your Google or Apple password)

2.2 Body and Physical Data

To provide personalized fitness and nutrition guidance, we collect:

  • Height, weight, age, and gender
  • Body measurements (e.g., waist, chest, arms)
  • Goal weight and fitness goals
  • Activity level and lifestyle information

2.3 Health Data (Apple Health / Google Health Connect)

With your explicit permission, we may read the following data from Apple Health or Google Health Connect:

  • Steps and distance walked/run
  • Active calories burned
  • Heart rate and resting heart rate
  • Sleep duration and stages
  • Blood oxygen saturation (SpO2)
  • Body fat percentage

Important: Health data access is entirely optional. You can connect or disconnect health data at any time in Settings. We only read health data — we never write to your Apple Health or Google Health Connect account. Health data is used solely to personalize your fitness insights and AI coaching. We never share health data with third parties for advertising, data brokering, or any purpose unrelated to providing our Services.

2.4 Nutrition and Food Data

  • Food logs — meals, snacks, and beverages you log manually or via barcode scanning
  • Meal photos — photos you take for AI-powered calorie analysis (stored securely in our database)
  • Nutrition tracking data — calories, macronutrients (protein, carbohydrates, fat), and daily summaries

2.5 Workout and Exercise Data

  • Exercise logs and workout history
  • Workout plans and programs
  • Progress tracking data (sets, reps, weights)

2.6 Photos

  • Progress photos — body photos you upload to track physical changes over time
  • Body transformation photos — photos submitted for AI-powered body prediction features
  • Meal photos — food images submitted for AI calorie analysis

Photos are stored in our secure database (Supabase Storage, EU). Meal and body photos are temporarily processed by our AI providers (see Section 4) and are not retained by those providers after processing.

2.7 AI Conversation Data

Conversations with Coach Flexx (our AI fitness coach) are stored to provide continuity and personalized coaching. Your AI chat history is associated with your account and is not shared with other users.

2.8 Device and Crash Data

We collect anonymous crash reports and performance data through Sentry to improve app stability. This data does not include personally identifiable information (PII). It may include device model, operating system version, and app state at the time of a crash.

2.9 Subscription Data

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. We receive purchase confirmation events (subscription type, status, and expiration date) but do not receive or store your payment card details, billing address, or other financial information.

5. Health Data — Special Protections

We treat health and body data with the highest level of care:

  • Health data from Apple Health and Google Health Connect is never shared with third parties for advertising, marketing, or data brokering.
  • Health data is used solely to provide fitness insights and personalized AI coaching within the App.
  • Health data access requires your explicit opt-in permission and can be revoked at any time.
  • We comply with Apple's Health data usage requirements and Google's Health Connect permissions policy.
  • We do not use health data to make eligibility determinations for insurance, employment, or lending.

6. Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored on Supabase servers located in Frankfurt, Germany (EU). This ensures compliance with GDPR data residency expectations for European users.

Security measures include:

  • Row Level Security (RLS) — enforced at the database level so that each user can only access their own data
  • Encrypted connections — all data transmitted between the App and our servers uses TLS/HTTPS encryption
  • Password hashing — passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (bcrypt); we never store plaintext passwords
  • Secure file storage — photos are stored in authenticated Supabase Storage buckets with per-user access controls
  • API key protection — server-side operations use service role keys that are never exposed to the client

7. Data Retention

  • Active account: Your data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Account deletion: When you delete your account, all data is permanently deleted from all database tables (30+ tables), all stored photos are removed, and your authentication record is erased. This process is irreversible.
  • Crash reports: Anonymous crash data in Sentry is retained for 90 days.
  • AI processing: Data sent to OpenAI and Stability AI for processing is not retained by those providers after the request is completed.
  • Subscription records: RevenueCat may retain purchase event data per their own retention policy, but this data does not include personal health or fitness information.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

8.1 Right to Access

You can view all your data within the App at any time (profile, workout history, nutrition logs, progress photos, etc.).

8.2 Right to Data Portability (Export)

You can export a complete copy of all your data in JSON format from the App's Settings. This includes profile information, body data, workout history, nutrition logs, AI chat history, and more. This fulfills GDPR Article 20 (Right to Data Portability).

8.3 Right to Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the App's Settings. Account deletion removes:

  • All personal information (name, email, body data)
  • All workout and nutrition data
  • All photos (meal photos, progress photos, body photos)
  • All AI conversation history
  • All health data synced from Apple Health / Google Health Connect
  • Your authentication record

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

8.4 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can revoke AI and data processing consent at any time in Settings. You can disconnect Apple Health or Google Health Connect at any time. You can stop using AI features at any time.

8.5 Right to Rectification

You can edit your profile information, body data, and other personal details at any time within the App.

8.6 Right to Restriction of Processing

You can disable specific data processing features (e.g., health data sync, AI features) while keeping your account active.

8.7 Right to Lodge a Complaint

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (supervisory authority) if you believe your data is being processed unlawfully.
