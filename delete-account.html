Delete your account & data

This page explains how to permanently delete your Flexx AI account and every piece of data linked to it. There are two ways — choose whichever suits you.

App: Flexx AI: AI Personal Trainer Developer: Flexx AI

Delete in the app Fastest · Instant

If you can still sign in, this removes everything immediately — no waiting.

  1. Open the Flexx AI app and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to Profile → Account Settings.
  3. Scroll to Danger Zone and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and all associated data are erased immediately and permanently.

Request by email If you can't sign in

Lost access to your account? Send us a request and we'll delete it for you.

Email flexxaiapp@gmail.com from the email address on your account, with the subject "Delete my account". We verify the request and complete deletion within 30 days.

Permanently deleted

  • Account & login credentials (email, password)
  • Profile & personal info (name, age, gender, measurements, goals)
  • Workouts, plans & exercise logs
  • Nutrition & food logs, meal history
  • Meal photos & progress photos
  • AI coach conversations
  • Health & fitness data (weight history, steps, activity)

Kept briefly, then removed

  • Purchase & transaction records we're legally required to retain for tax and accounting — kept only for the period the law requires, then deleted.
  • Anonymized, aggregated data that can no longer identify you.

Retention: In-app deletion is immediate. Email requests are completed within 30 days. Legally required transaction records are the only exception and are deleted as soon as the mandatory retention period ends.
